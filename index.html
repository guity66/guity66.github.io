The True Meaning Of Christmas

With no true origin, Christmas is a holiday of giving and recieving. The true meaning can be interpretted in many ways. One may suggest it's a time to be around family, others may say it's a time simply to think of others rather than oneself. One things for certain, the Christmas season will always be the best time of the year.
